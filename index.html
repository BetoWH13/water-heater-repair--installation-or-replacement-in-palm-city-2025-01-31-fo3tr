Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my water heater?

Annual maintenance is recommended for optimal performance and longevity. However, commercial systems may require bi-annual inspections depending on usage.

What are the signs I need water heater repair?

Watch for insufficient hot water, unusual noises, leaks, rusty water, or inconsistent temperatures. These symptoms indicate potential issues requiring professional attention.

How long do water heaters typically last?

With proper maintenance, traditional water heaters last 8-12 years, while tankless systems can last 20+ years. Regular maintenance is key to maximizing lifespan.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency service throughout Palm City. Our technicians are always ready to respond to urgent situations.

Are your services covered by warranty?

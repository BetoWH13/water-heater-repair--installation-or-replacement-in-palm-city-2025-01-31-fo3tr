5 Critical Signs Your Water Heater Needs Professional Repair

January 31, 2025

Is your water heater showing signs of trouble? Learn the five critical indicators that signal the need for professional repair. From unusual rumbling sounds caused by sediment buildup to rusty water indicating internal corrosion, these warning signs shouldn't be ignored. Watch out for inconsistent water temperatures that could mean failing components, and address any leaks immediately to prevent water damage. With most units lasting 8-12 years, age-related decline in performance might signal it's time for maintenance or replacement. Don't wait for complete system failure - Palm City's expert technicians are ready to help with comprehensive inspections, repairs, and maintenance solutions.
